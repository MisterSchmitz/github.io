Story

A bit about how I arrived here

  • 1993-2006

    It's in My Blood

    I've been a math geek since I could first add and subtract. My first experience with programming was on a TI-83+ calculator. I made casino games.

  • 2006-2010

    Engineering School

    Busted my butt through college to earn my Engineering degree from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

  • 2010-2014

    Consulting

    I wanted to work in the industry - and my Technical Consulting gig bridged the gap between Business and Technology.

  • Present

    Product Management

    I discovered Product Management and loved that I could exercise my creativity while playing a more strategic role for my company. In turn I learned the best practices of agile software development.

  • Present

    Full-stack Web Development

    The Engineer within me keeps coming back. I want to develop amazing software. I've been heavily focused on broadening my development skillset.
